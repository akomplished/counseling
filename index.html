Naviance Family Connection

Online College and Career resource that students can access using their Student Connection username and password.

Naviance provides College and Career readiness solutions that will assist you in planning your future goals by connecting your academic achievements to your post-secondary aspirations. At Moorpark High School Naviance provides students access to request College and Career related items such as:

  • Transcripts
  • Letters of Recommendation
  • College Applications
  • Career Assessment Surveys
  • College and Career Research Tools

Transcript Fees
  • Transcripts for Current Students are a flat fee of $10, paid to the Student Store.

  • Transcripts for Graduates are $5 each, paid to the Registrar's Office.

Community Service Hours

As a graduation requirement students are expected to earn 40 hours of Community Service through various pre-approved programs.

The Counseling Office has many community service opportunities listed on our announcement board. If your student still has not completed the 40 hours required for graduation please encourage them to look in our office. This form needs to be submitted to your students’ counselor once the hours are completed and all signatures have been acquired. Any senior suspended during their senior year must complete an additional 20 hours of community service to be eligible for the graduation ceremony.

Seniors must have their "Form D” turned in by May 25, 2017 to be eligible for graduation.
