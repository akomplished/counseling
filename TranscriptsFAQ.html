Transcripts Questions?
How much do Official Transcripts cost?
  • Official Transcripts cost a single flat fee of $10 paid at the Student Store.
How do I send my transcripts to the Colleges I applied to?
  • University of California (UC) and/or California State University (CSU) Colleges:
  • During the application process if the UC or CSU campus requests a transcript you would first pay at Student Store the flat fee.
  • Then show the receipt you receive to Moorpark High School's Registrar Ms. Montgomery. She will provide you with a sealed hard copy of your official transcript for you to mail. It is important you do not open this sealed envelope.
  • If you plan to attend a CSU or UC campus you will need to send a Final Transcript after you graduate. Final Transcripts will be available through the Registrar’s Office approximately two weeks after graduation. These are not sent via Naviance Family Connection.
  • Private, Out of State, and/or Common Application Colleges:
  • The application process for these Colleges is handled through the Naviance Family Connection website
  • After you request transcripts through Naviance be sure you pay the flat fee for your transcripts through the Student Store.
  • Initial, Mid-Year and Final Transcripts will able be handled automatically by your Counselor.
  • Your Final Transcript will be sent to the school you indicate on your Senior Survey.
